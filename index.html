Mobile App for
Creative Collaboration

Hexterity is here to offer you an easy way to connect with creative people and work on projects together. Do you need a photographer or a guitarist to work with?
Swipe through the database of our users, see their portfolio and choose the most suitable one for you and your work.

"It is the perfect idea that connects people and enhances creativity."

Find Others

Through our mobile application you can find professionals and enthusiast from the Industries that you are interested in. Simply open the app, select the desired field and you will be able to see their work.

Meet Up

After selecting the Industry, you can set up the distance you are willing to travel to meet them. For example, you can decide to meet the Photographers in the 3 kilometer radius from your House. We will also suggest the best places to meet at.

Collaborate

Once you exchange a few messages to arrange the meeting and find a cozy public spot (it's all for your safety!), it's time to meet up and discuss your artistic collaboration, payed freelance work or just an exchange of skills.

Search

Other artists and creatives by their location, a particular skillset or keyword.

Create

Profile page with your portfolio, where you showcase the work you are the most proud of.

Meet

New people and start networking in the industry you have always dreamt of being a part of.

Exchange

Skills and tips with other artists that can be valuable to your personal or work project.

Share

Ideas, ideals, brainstorm and who knows, maybe you will be the inventor of a product that makes the world a better place.

Explore

Choose from the range of places we are partners with and find the best new hot spots in town.

What makes us unique

Creativity has been a buzz word for a few years now and Art is now regarded more than ever. When making this mobile application, the main thing we had in mind though was to enable people in their local area with the same interests while also supporting local businesses. These three pillars are the main oundations of our concept and are also the thing that differenciate us from anything on the market.
